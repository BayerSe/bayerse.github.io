Biography

Sebastian Bayer is a data scientist and AI consultant at the Robert Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence where he works on solving challenging problems of Bosch-internal customers. He is currently concentrating on topics involving mathematical optimization and statistical techniques and modelling. For the last two years he has been working in collaboration with the Robert Bosch Wafer and Sensor Fab in Reutlingen, where he is replacing rule-based dispatching with optimized scheduling routines. He holds an M.Sc. in Economics and a Ph.D. in Econometrics from the University of Konstanz, where he focused on applying statistical and mathematical models on modeling financial market risks. His interests include combinatorial optimization, operations research, predictive modelling, and programming
